Help

The default ("create") page provides a simple interface to design your electronic greeting card. The message text, card image, background color, and sender and receiver names can all be customized. Your card will appear in an automatically-updating preview as you begin to edit it. Press the "Toggle preview" button to turn off the preview feature.

All your customizations are encoded in the URL, so the card contents will not expire or change. Even if the site goes down, you can retrieve your card by downloading the generator and importing the link.

The card generation and retrieval process is done entirely locally, so none of the card contents are stored on any server.
